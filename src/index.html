About Wagonkered

Based in the South-West, Wagonkered is a trendy bar solution available for hire for your next event. We are fully licensed and insured and can cater for any occasion whether it be a full blown party, wedding or festival. We have many available options and can provide a tailored experience for you event: Black Tie, Fancy Dress, Oktoberfest and more...

Johnny, the Volkswagen cab

The bar is a fully road legal 1970 low light single cab VW imported from Italy in 2014. Johnny, as we have named the him, has been totally re-built, re-sprayed, re-trimmed and is now a fully functional bar created in the back, serving a range of alcholic and soft drinks.
